FESTIVALS IN TELANGANA

  • Telangana is a state which recently came into existence; earlier, it was in Andhra Pradesh. It was separated from Andhra Pradesh because of the Telugu-speaking culture.
  • Telangana is the meeting point of North India and South India, which have diverse languages and cultures. The capital of Telangana is Hyderabad, known as a ‘miniature India,’ full of diversity and culture.
